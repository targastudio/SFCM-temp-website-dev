[studio for cosmopolitical models] is an architecture practice based in Lisbon. Founded in 2025 by Raffaele Sarubbo, and led together with Marco Falcone and Gianluca Sartin, the practice develops research and design approaches that investigate architecture as a field of negotiation between heterogeneous forces. Its work explores the models that shape collective behaviors and forms of organization, with the aim of critically analyzing them, questioning them, and, when necessary, imagining alternative scenarios.
